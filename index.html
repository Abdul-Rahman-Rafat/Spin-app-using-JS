How to play?

1. Deposit an amount to start playing.

2. Set your bet per line using the 'Bet / line' input.

3. Click the 'Spin' button to spin the reels.

4. The game will automatically calculate your winnings
Based on the symbols that align on the paylines.

5. You can adjust the number of active paylines
By clicking the 'x1' button.

6. Enjoy the game and try your luck!
